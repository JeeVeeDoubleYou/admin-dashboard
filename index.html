Reintroduction Programs

Teaching zoo animals to be wild again: Because who needs the safety of daily meals and health checks when you can dodge poachers and look for food in a shrinking habitat?

Volunteer Programs

Unpaid labor, the backbone of our zoo! Sign up to scoop poop, clean cages, and do the jobs no one else wants—all for the warm, fuzzy feeling of ‘helping out.’
